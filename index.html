Talking about the movie

Rorschach is a 2022 Indian Malayalam-language neo-noir psychological thriller film directed by Nissam Basheer and produced by Mammootty under, Mammootty Kampany.[2] It stars Mammootty, Sharafudheen, Jagadish, Grace Antony, Bindu Panicker, Kottayam Nazeer, Sanju Sivram, Asif Ali and George Abraham. The film's music was composed by Midhun Mukundan and cinematography was handled by Nimish Ravi.
